# The Flag

The flag is the object that decides the game. Every player has one. Finding and capturing your opponent's flag — and returning it safely to your own fortification — is how you win.

## Your Flag

Your flag begins the game carried by one of your crew members aboard your starting ship. At some point in the early game, you need to decide where to hide it.

Hiding your flag is a deliberate action. When your flag-carrying crew member is on a land hex, you can choose to hide the flag there. It takes 1 turn. The flag disappears from your crew member and is placed on that hex, invisible to your opponent.

You can always see your own hidden flag. You can also pick it up again — if you change your mind about its location, move a crew member to the hex, pick up the flag, and hide it somewhere else.

## Choosing a Hiding Place

You can hide your flag on any land hex you have explored, including inside a live fortification. A flag inside a fortification benefits from the fort's cannon fire if an enemy crew tries to reach it.

A remote, unexplored-looking corner of the map is unpredictable. A flag inside a fortification is well-defended. Both choices are valid strategies.

## The Enemy Flag

You do not know where your opponent has hidden their flag. To find it, you must explore.

The enemy flag does not appear at normal sight ranges. Your crew must move to a hex directly adjacent to the hidden flag before it becomes visible to you. Moving through the general area is not enough — you need to search carefully.

When you discover the enemy flag, move your crew unit onto that hex to capture it. The capture is automatic — no separate action is needed.

## Carrying the Captured Flag

Once you capture the enemy flag, the crew member who captured it carries it. That unit is now your most important unit in the game.

Your goal is to move the carrier back to one of your live fortifications. When the carrier enters a friendly fortification, you win.

**Warning:** If the crew member carrying the enemy flag is destroyed, you lose the game immediately. Protect your carrier at all costs during the return journey.
