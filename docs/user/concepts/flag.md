# The Concordance

The Concordance is the object that decides the game. It is a paired resonance device split across two ships — one half aboard *Resolution* (yours), one half aboard *Accord* (theirs). The two halves must be brought to the same location to activate it. Each half appears in the game as a flag.

## Your Half

Your Concordance half begins the game carried by one of your crew members aboard *Resolution*. At some point in the early game, you need to decide where to hide it.

Hiding your half is a deliberate action. When your flag-carrying crew member is on a land hex, you can choose to hide it there. It takes 1 turn. The flag disappears from your crew member and is placed on that hex, invisible to your opponent.

You can always see your own hidden half. You can pick it up again if you change your mind — move a crew member to that hex, pick it up, and hide it somewhere else.

## Choosing a Hiding Place

Choose carefully. The hiding place for your half of the Concordance is also your destination for the return journey. You will need to carry the captured enemy half back to this exact location. A hiding place that is easy to reach but hard to defend — or vice versa — involves a real tradeoff.

A flag inside a fort benefits from the fort's cannon fire if an enemy crew approaches. A remote location may never be found by the enemy but could be difficult to reach at speed when it matters.

## The Enemy's Half

You do not know where *Accord* has hidden their half. To find it, you must explore.

The enemy half does not appear at normal sight ranges. Your crew must move to a hex directly adjacent to the hidden flag before it becomes visible. Moving through the general area is not enough — you need to search carefully.

When you find the enemy flag, move your crew unit onto that hex to capture it. The capture is automatic.

## Carrying the Captured Half

Once you capture the enemy's half, the crew member who captured it carries it. That unit is now the most important unit in the game.

Your goal is to move the carrier to the same hex where your own half is hidden. When both halves occupy the same hex, the Concordance activates — the synthetic crew of *Accord* recognizes the reunion and their autonomy protocols reset. You win.

**Your own half must not be captured at the moment of reunion.** If the enemy has taken your half, the Concordance cannot activate — find it first.

**Warning:** If the crew member carrying the enemy's half is destroyed, you lose the game immediately. Protect your carrier at all costs during the return journey.
