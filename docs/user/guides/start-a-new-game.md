# How to Start a New Game

This guide explains how to start a new game of Windfall and what to expect in the first few turns.

## Before You Begin

Windfall runs in a modern desktop browser. No installation is required. Open the game page in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge on a laptop or desktop computer.

A new game generates a unique world map. This takes a moment — expect up to 2 seconds after the page loads.

## Steps

1. Open the Windfall page in your browser.
2. Select **New Game** from the main menu.
3. Wait for the world to generate. The map is created fresh for each game.
4. The game begins. The world is black — entirely undiscovered. Your starting ship appears at the edge of your visible area, surrounded by a small patch of revealed ocean and coastline.

## Your Starting Position

You begin with:
- One ship
- A crew of several members aboard the ship
- One flag, carried by one of your crew

Your opponent — the AI player — starts on the far side of the map with the same resources.

## What to Do First

Your first priority is exploration. Sail along coastlines to reveal terrain. You are looking for land with a combination of useful features: stone for defense, grassland and forest for production, and a natural shape that will reduce how many wall segments you need to build.

Your second priority is hiding your flag. Do not leave it aboard your ship forever — a ship is a mobile target. When you find a promising piece of land, consider landing crew and hiding the flag in a location that will be hard to find.

You do not need to build a fort before hiding the flag. A remote, unexplored-looking hex can be a valid hiding spot. You can always pick the flag up and move it later.

## Result

After completing these steps, your game is underway. You have a ship exploring the world and a flag hidden somewhere defensible. The real game — building, conflict, and the race to find the enemy flag — has begun.
